NCT DREAM

NCT Dream is a South Korean boy group formed by SM Entertainment and is a part of the larger NCT (Neo Culture Technology) project. Debuting in 2016, NCT Dream was initially positioned as the youth sub-unit of NCT, known for their fresh and energetic concepts. Comprising talented and versatile members, NCT Dream has captured hearts with their vibrant performances, catchy music, and youthful charm. The group's music often reflects themes of teenage dreams, growth, and friendship, resonating with fans worldwide. Known for their dynamic choreographies and engaging stage presence, NCT Dream has delivered hits such as "Chewing Gum," "Boom," "Ridin'," and "Hot Sauce," showcasing their evolution and versatility as artists. As one of the leading acts in the K-pop scene, NCT Dream continues to captivate audiences with their unique blend of pop, hip-hop, and R&B, solidifying their status as a powerhouse in the industry.
