2NE1

2NE1 was a groundbreaking South Korean girl group formed by YG Entertainment in 2009. Comprising members CL, Bom, Dara, and Minzy, they quickly became a force in the K-pop industry with their bold style, fierce performances, and genre-defying music. Known for hit songs like "Fire," "I Am the Best," and "Come Back Home," 2NE1 was celebrated for their empowering lyrics and innovative music videos. They were pioneers in fashion, often setting trends with their edgy and eclectic looks. Despite their disbandment in 2016, 2NE1's impact on K-pop remains profound, with a legacy that continues to inspire fans and artists worldwide.
