INTRODUCTION

Welcome to KPOP TIME CAPSULE, an archival hub dedicated to preserving the legacy of K-pop groups throughout history. Here, we meticulously collect and categorize K-pop groups into distinct generations, offering a clear timeline of their evolution within the industry.


Our classification method divides these groups into six generations, allowing visitors to explore how each era is defined by unique styles, concepts, and influences. Delving into our archive reveals intriguing parallels between K-pop groups within each generation, illustrating how Korean pop music trends have been shaped by the global popular culture landscape.


As the K-pop phenomenon continues to grow, it embraces inclusivity and diversity, welcoming talents from various backgrounds, including individuals of different races. The emergence of virtual groups further underscores the profound impact of technology on shaping popular culture.


We extend our appreciation to all K-pop artists for their invaluable contributions to the world of music. Our hope is that this website serves as a source of inspiration for visitors to delve deeper into the world of K-pop, prompting thoughtful reflections on the evolution of popular culture and its influence on public ideologies.
